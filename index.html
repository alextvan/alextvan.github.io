projects

Jackpot Miners

Over the course of Summer 2023, I was given the opportunity to work with NAVER Z (ZEPETO), a virtual mobile gaming platform. Throughout 3-4 months, I was taught Unity essentials and had to use said Unity to develop a game on the ZEPETO platform. At the end of the program, I had the great opportunity to make Jackpot Miners, a game where players would mine and collect blocks to then exchange and sell them for perks and upgrades. This game was programmed via TypeScript.

Frontier Fugue Fighters

Frontier Fugue Fighters is a desktop rhythm-fighting game where two players have to perfectly time and synchronize their battle actions to the rhythm! This game was made in game engine Godot using GDScript and was made for the Bitcamp Hackathon 2023.

Bytegotchi

Bytegotchi is a Tamagotchi-like desktop game where players have to take care of their Bitcamp character! Players will have to feed and wash their character to keep the game from ending. In addition, players have to control their character using a replicated controller. This game was made in Processing IDE and Arduino and was made for Bitcamp Hackathon 2022.

Pong

Pong was the first ever game I developed. It was developed as part of a school project. The game was made in Processing IDE during Spring of 2022.
